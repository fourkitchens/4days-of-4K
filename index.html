#4DaysOf4K

DrupalCon like a local.

Let us be your host during your stay in Austin. Each day of DrupalCon Austin, Four Kitchens will have something fun for you to do.

Be notified when new events are added. Join our mailing list!

Subscribe me, pls!

Monday 6/2

Office Space screening at the world-famous Alamo Drafthouse!

Yeaaahhh, we're going to need you to come in on Monday... and join us for a special Drupal screening of Office Space at the Alamo Drafthouse. The Drafthouse is known for its unique film experience. This is sure to be a great way to kick-off DrupalCon. Space is limited, so please RSVP in advance. Once tickets are gone, they are gone!

SOLD OUT!

Tuesday 6/3

Private dinner at Franklin BBQ

7–9pm

Bon Appetitie magaizine named Franklin BBQ the best BBQ in America. We're throwing a special, private dinner at Franklin BBQ for our clients, partners, and friends. Space is very limited and by invitation only.

SOLD OUT!

Drupal Karaoke Night

10pm–? at Swan Dive (615 Red River St)

If you are not able to make it to dinner at Franklin, join us for an unofficial karaoke night at Swan Dive! You can check out the song list in advance, thanks to Diamond Karaoke.

No RSVP, just show up!

Wednesday 6/4

Party with Pantheon and New Relic

6–10pm at Banger's

Join Pantheon, New Relic, and Pantheon Partners Alley Interactive, Ciplex, DOOR3, Hook42, Kalamuna, Mediacurrent, Phase2, ThinkShout, and US! There will be a live band, games, great draft beer, and delicious artisanal sausage!

RSVP now!

Thursday 6/5

Lunch with Four Kitchens and the future of CMS

Noon–1pm at Capital Factory, lunch provided w/ RSVP

Have lunch on us, and let's have a discussion about the future of CMS. Get ready for some BIG, innovative ideas. Space is very limited, so please RSVP in advance.

RSVP now!

Friday 6/6

Unofficial Closing Night Happy Hour

5–7pm at Craft Pride (61 Rainey St)

It's sprint day! Join us for an unofficial happy hour at Craft Pride! Craft Pride offers over 50 local Texas beers on tap. After a long day working hard making Drupal better, we know you'll deserve a cold one.

No RSVPs required, just bring your lovely self!
